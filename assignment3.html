Major in Creative Technologies

The interdisciplinary Creative Technologies major emphasizes design and practice in the integration of digital technologies and the fine arts. In addition to foundational study across the fine arts, the major provides training and experiences across a range of creative/technical fields including video, sound, electronic music, music production, gaming, motion graphics, interactivity, AR/VR, UI/UX, mobile, web, and computer programming concepts.

Major in Business Administration (M.B.A)

A Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a graduate degree that focuses on business management and leadership skills. It can help you advance your career, start a business, or move into a new industry.

Organizational Behavior

The study of how people behave in organizations, and how those behaviors affect the organization's success.

It's a social science field that draws on psychology, sociology, and other disciplines to create theories about human behavior in organizations.
